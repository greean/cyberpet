Cyber-pet: Gizmo

How to play

You have been given 'Gizmo', a small furry creature known as a Mogwai, to look after as a pet.

Mogwai's are not easy to look after, but if you can increase Gizmo's health and happyness you will prove yourself as a great Mogwai carer.

However, if you are not up to the task and Gizmo's health or happyness falls too low, the shopkeeper will come and take him back.

Choose from the options available in order to care for Gizmo.

Good Luck!
